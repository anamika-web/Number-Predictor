Number Predictor

  • You will have to think of a number from 1-1000.
  • Thereafter you will be asked regarding the number you've thought of.
  • You can respond to the questions in either yes or no.
  • You must not answer incorrectly about the question otherwise the predictor will not be able to guess your number correctly.
  • After you've responded to all the questions your number will be revealed and you can see if the guess is correct or not.
